# Stripped Down: High ROI Features Built Around Location

---

## The Core Product in One Sentence

**A forum where you already know something real about people, plus location to find them near you.**

That's it. Everything else is secondary.

---

## The Stack Ranked By ROI

### 1. Location + Profile From Forum History (Build This First)

This is your entire moat in one feature.

When someone opens the location view they see nearby users but each card shows:
- One photo
- Which communities they post in
- One recent post or comment they wrote

That's it. No stats, no laundry list of interests.

**Why this is high ROI:**
- You already have the forum data, you're just surfacing it differently
- Instant differentiation from Grindr - you know something real about this person before you message
- Minimal build - location layer on top of existing profiles

**Build time:** 4-6 weeks for a competent small team
**Competitive impact:** Immediate and obvious to users

---

### 2. Simple Status Signal

One toggle on the home screen:

**"Open to meet"** - on or off

When it's on:
- You appear in the location view
- You see others who are also on
- It auto-turns off after 4 hours

**Why this is high ROI:**
- Solves the core Grindr anxiety of not knowing if someone actually wants to connect right now
- Opt-in means people who appear are actually available - higher conversion to real conversations
- Technically trivial to build
- Creates a daily active usage habit - people toggle it when they go out

**Build time:** 1 week
**Competitive impact:** High - Grindr has no equivalent, you're always "on" there whether you want to be or not

---

### 3. One Conversation Starter Mechanic

When you tap someone in the location view you see their profile and one prompt:

**"Start with something you have in common"**

The app suggests: "You both post in [community]" or "You both commented on [topic recently]"

One tap sends a pre-written opener with that context. They see it, they can reply or ignore.

**Why this is high ROI:**
- Solves the blank message box problem that kills conversion on every dating app
- Uses your existing forum data as the engine
- Higher response rates mean users feel the app works, retention goes up
- No AI needed, just a simple matching query

**Build time:** 2 weeks
**Competitive impact:** Very high - this is the thing users will tell their friends about

---

### 4. DMs Gated by Mutual Forum Activity

Not open DMs. You can message someone if:
- You both post in at least one same community, OR
- They have "open to meet" toggled on

**Why this is high ROI:**
- Dramatically reduces harassment with zero moderation overhead
- Users feel safer = higher retention especially for users who've left Grindr due to harassment
- Creates an organic incentive to actually participate in forums (to unlock messaging)
- This incentive loop grows your core forum engagement as a side effect

**Build time:** 1 week (it's just a permission check)
**Competitive impact:** High for retention, especially female and nonbinary users in your community

---

## What to Cut Completely (For Now)

| Feature | Why Cut |
|---|---|
| Daily match algorithm | High build cost, needs data volume you may not have yet |
| Video/voice chat | Infrastructure cost, moderation risk |
| Events system | Complex, distraction from core |
| Reputation scoring | Needs time and data to be meaningful |
| Subscription tiers | Get users first, monetize second |

---

## The Actual MVP

```
Forum (existing)
    +
Location view showing nearby users with forum context
    +
Open to meet toggle
    +
Context-aware conversation starter
    +
Gated DMs
```

That's your product. Four additions to what you already have.

---

## Why This Beats Grindr on Its Own Terms

Grindr's weakness isn't the location feature. It's that **location is all it has.**

You have location plus actual knowledge of who someone is. That's a better product for anyone who's ever messaged someone on Grindr and had nothing to say after "hey."

---

## First Metric to Watch

Not downloads. Not signups.

**Conversation rate:** Of people who view someone's profile in the location view, what percentage send a message.

If that number is higher than Grindr's equivalent (which is very low - most views result in nothing) you have product market fit. Everything else is optimization.
